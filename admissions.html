Why SCS?

Seoul Christian School is focused on providing an academically rigorous, biblically-focused curriculum that covers a wide range of subjects in order to prepare your child for college, the workplace, and their career.

We are especially interested in teaching students to be competent in English so that they have a wider range of academic and employment opportunities available to them when they graduate. We offer an ESL program as well as a full English curriculum for each student.

Our one-to-one program pairs each student with a laptop, allowing us to teach them to effectively use technology in their education. This helps to equip them both for a stronger education and for careers in a networked world.

Starting the Process

If you think that the Lord is leading your family to Seoul Christian School, please contact our admissions team to set up an appointment. You'll have the opportunity to see the facilities, meet faculty members, and observe the student body of Seoul Christian School.

We look forward to hearing from you! Feel free to call our office at 2876-3969 or email our admissions counselor.

After SCS?

Our students graduate with a solid academic foundation which they can build on at a number of universities around the world. Our faculty work with the students to assist them in applying to schools and are more than happy to provide letters of recommendation.

90% of Seoul Christian School graduates successfully apply for and enter college immediately after graduation, and the remaining 10% enter college after taking a gap year. We have graduates studying and working in Korea, the United States, Japan, and the United Kingdom.

We also are developing a tracked program for students who plan to attend Korean colleges or universities that will allow them to take their exams by the time they graduate, instead of needing to take an additional year for study.
